    Prior Experience and Education

    Prior to The Iron Yard, John spent 10+ years working in the transportation planning and engineering sector including at the Metropolitan Atlanta Rapid Transit Authority, the Transit Planning Board - a joint-venture of MARTA, the Atlanta Regional Commission (ARC), and the Georgia Regional Transportation Authority, and several private transportation consulting firms. John specialized in working on corridor analysis, organizational management and structure, performance measurements, real estate management, network analysis and Transit Oriented Development. John has a variety of experience including:
